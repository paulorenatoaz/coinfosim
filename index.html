CoInfoSim — Published Research Reports

CoInfoSim is a research simulator for evaluating cooperative advantage among information channels in supervised classification. It studies when channel subsets outperform isolated or simpler subsets and how many labeled samples are needed before those gains emerge.

Scientific question: When does cooperation among information channels improve supervised classification?

Dataset-anchored protocol

Dataset-anchored scenarios compare Real → Real, Single Gaussian → Real, and GMM → Real. All three arms are evaluated on the same fixed real test set.

The reports examine classification loss, channel-subset rankings, cooperative thresholds N*, ranking fidelity, winner agreement, and progressive N-star similarity.

Implemented datasets

Occupancy Detection

A five-channel room-occupancy classification study using Temperature, Humidity, Light, CO₂, and Humidity Ratio.

UCI Air Quality

A five-channel study based on the UCI Air Quality PT08 metal-oxide sensor responses. The C6H6(GT) benzene reference defines the binary target and is excluded from classifier input.

Dataset DOI: 10.24432/C59K5F
